How it works

One-time customer setup

Your customer enters their payment details securely online. Authorise you to collect payment by Direct Debit. You can add the customisable payment page directly to your website or send them a secure link.

Schedule Payments

Set up a flexible payment schedule that works for your customers and your business. Choose the frequency and dates for payments to be automatically debited from your customers' accounts. Ensure timely payments without any hassle

Get paid automatically

Once your customer authorizes, payments are collected automatically on the scheduled dates. No need for manual intervention, ensuring you get paid on time every time, improving cash flow and reducing administrative work.

Keep track

Easily monitor all transactions through your dashboard. View payment statuses, upcoming payments, and any failed transactions. Get real-time insights and detailed reports to keep track of your finances effortlessly.
